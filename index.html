Essay · Passa vs ARY

“Passa gold is refined at the jeweller's bench. ARY gold is refined at a lab.”

The passa rate — literally "rawa passa" — is 24K only by local convention. Because it is refined at the shop, its true purity sits closer to 23.8K. It is the benchmark most Pakistani jewellers quote, but it is not the rate to invest in.

ARY certified bars are lab assayed at 999.0 purity, sealed and serialised. They carry a small premium over passa gold — that premium is the cost of certainty. For long-term holding, choose ARY.
